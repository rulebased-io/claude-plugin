Combine multiple notes into a synthesis that extracts patterns and insights.

Searches brain for related notes → presents candidates for confirmation → extracts key points → creates `notes/synthesis-<topic>.md` with overview, key insights, tensions, gaps, and sources.
Adds backlinks to all source notes. Never hallucinate — only synthesize from existing notes. Minimum 2 source notes required.
