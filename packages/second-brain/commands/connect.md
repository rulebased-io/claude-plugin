Find hidden connections between notes and suggest bidirectional wiki-links.

Three modes: connect a specific note (rank by shared tags/keywords, top 5), orphan scan (find notes with zero links), cluster discovery (suggest new MOCs).

Links are always bidirectional. Add to both `links` frontmatter and inline `[[wiki-links]]`. Never delete existing links.
