# Areas

Ongoing responsibilities you maintain over time. Unlike projects, areas have no end date.

## Examples

- Health & fitness
- Career development
- Financial management
- Side projects (ongoing)

## Guidelines

- Areas are maintained, not completed
- Review areas quarterly — are they still relevant?
- If an area becomes inactive, move to `archives/`
- Keep area notes atomic — one topic per file
